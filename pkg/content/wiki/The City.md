Cosmopolitan enough that a [[bird-folk]] merchant draws no more notice than any other commuter, [[the City]] organises its inter-district movement with a firm hand — private vehicles are confined to designated transit corridors or must yield to the [[public transit]] network within city limits, the prohibition on unrestricted [[sliding]] being less a courtesy than a security doctrine. Those willing to do their sliding beyond the city boundary can equip a vehicle with the necessary [[slide device]] for around two gold pieces, though the vehicles themselves run closer to two thousand — an asymmetry that says something pointed about who, exactly, the City expects to be travelling in comfort.
