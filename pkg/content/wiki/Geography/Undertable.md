---
title: Undertable
---
A narrow, squished shop wedged between two buildings in a space too large for an alleyway but too small for a standard building. The Undertable has no shop-front windows, and its entrance is marked only by a door bearing a handwritten sign reading *under table* alongside a crude drawing of a table.

The interior is a cramped room filled with tables stacked high with miscellaneous goods, including kitchen appliances, loose jewelry, radios in various states of deconstruction, and a large number of firearms.
