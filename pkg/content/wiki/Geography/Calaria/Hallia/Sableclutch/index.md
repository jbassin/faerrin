Sableclutch is a poorer neighborhood at the southernmost point of [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/index|Hallia]]. Hugging the south bank of the Fousan River, Sableclutch is dominated by the dockworkers and warehouse employees that ply their trade on the river. The district is somewhat overlooked by the rest of the capital -- whilst many of the goods that enter into the city start their journey in Sableclutch, the power centers of the [[Org/index|Orgs]] that manage it are found elsewhere.

The streets of Sableclutch are winding and largely ungoverned — traffic enforcement is absent, as there is no clear party willing to fund it. Trucks carting goods in and out of factories and scrapyards are a constant presence, particularly near the district's trolley station. Goblinoid creatures are commonly employed as factory workers and are a visible part of the working population.

Sableclutch is also notable for its numerous religious areas, including many churches. The majority of these are dedicated to the [[Iridescent Host]], with a smattering of other gods also represented.

### Notable Locations

**Sableclutch Scrap** is a scrapyard operating within the neighborhood. It is run by an old friend of [[Anouk Marchal]] and regularly takes on couriers and other workers.
