Sableclutch is a poorer neighborhood at the southernmost point of [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/index|Hallia]]. Hugging the south bank of the Fousan River, Sableclutch is dominated by the dockworkers and warehouse employees that ply their trade on the river. The district is somewhat overlooked by the rest of the capital -- whilst many of the goods that enter into the city start their journey in Sableclutch, the power centers of the [[Org/index|Orgs]] that manage it are found elsewhere.


### Sableclutch Scrap

Sableclutch Scrap is a large scrapyard and junkyard business located within the neighborhood. It is an expansive site featuring mountains of trash and industrial equipment, including a crane-like device used to flatten vehicles. The yard is run by a proprietor known as [[Copperjaw]], and has a reputation for giving jobs to anyone willing to do the work.

### Character of the District

The streets of Sableclutch are relatively winding. The district contains factories and scrapyards, with trucks regularly carting goods in and out of them, and is served by a trolley station. Goblinoid creatures are commonly employed as workers in the local factories and can be seen commuting through the streets in workman's attire. The area lacks traffic cops — a reflection of the district's neglected status, as there is no clear party willing to fund them.
