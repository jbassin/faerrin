Young even by humanoid reckoning, Argyle carries himself with the quiet authority of someone whose [[Scale]]-issued business cards announce his doctor's licence in bold — a perk funded, quietly, by his parents [[Aaron Cross]] and [[Joran Davinrock]], both operatives of the [[Scale]] whose precise roles remain classified. His eyes, ordinarily gold, have a habit of shifting toward bright white or a cold blue, which tends to unsettle people who notice.
