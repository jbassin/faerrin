Half-elven and border-bred, Johnny came up in [[Helmstier]] — a small mountain town pressed against the edge of [[Raelion]] — where he ran [[The Last Stop]], a bar whose name did double duty as both a traveller's landmark and a closing-time joke. His path to [[Iconoclasm]] was eased by an existing familiarity with [[Smiler]] and more than a few prior visits to the [[Undertable]], though what he carried with him from that mountain threshold was something quieter — a melancholy poem he composed for [[Anzu]], about a blackbird making its life inside a library.
