title: Smiler
---
Smiler is adorned with extensive jewelry: multiple yellowish metal bands on both arms, gems braided into his tail, flare gauges in his ears with gems embedded in them, and a long beard braided in the same style as his tail with gems throughout.

Smiler is acquainted with [[Org/The Harlequins/People/Donnie|Donnie]] and [[Org/The Harlequins/People/Red|Red]], and is willing to pass messages between them.
