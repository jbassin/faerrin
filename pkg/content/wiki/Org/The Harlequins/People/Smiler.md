Smiler is a centaur standing approximately nine feet tall at full height, forcing him to stoop beneath the low ceiling of his establishment. He is the proprietor of the [[Undertable]] and operates as a [[fence]], dealing in both buying and selling goods.

Smiler is known to [[Johnny]] and extends a friends discount to Johnny's associates as a personal favor, on the basis that friends of his friend will not inform on him to law enforcement. His pawn shop stocks items such as poly tools, priced at nine gold pieces.
