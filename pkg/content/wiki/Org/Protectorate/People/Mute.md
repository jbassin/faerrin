Mute is the partner of [[Org/Protectorate/People/Officer Smith|Officer Smith]], a fellow operative who is unable to speak due to a bad accident sustained in a scuffle with a local gang. In place of speech, Mute communicates only through a gargling sound.

Like Smith, Mute is a shapeshifting creature capable of disguising itself as an ordinary office worker to blend into urban environments. When dropping this disguise, Mute grows approximately a foot and a half in size, and its clothing melts back into its body. In their true form, both Mute and Smith appear as large, pink, fleshy, naked mole rat-like creatures, with eyes that sink into featureless sockets and most other facial features dissolving back into the skin.
