Kadou Floch is a wire-thin, sharp-featured figure of human and elven heritage who moves with an uncanny fluid grace. He favors a dark green coat with synthetic silk and keeps a monocle over his right eye that constantly clicks through its lenses, his fingertips permanently stained amber from his work.

Kadou is a member of [[Org/Alkahest Freight/index|Alkahest Freight]].
