An automobile designer and builder. Based in [[Geography/Brithwyn/index|Brithwyn]], Chorath makes cars for any occasion -- city driving, shootouts, and exploring the [[Infinite Horizon|Horizon]].

That [[Chorath Engineering]]'s name carries more weight in dining rooms than in garages owes everything to the [[Chorath Guide]] — a publication that began as a motorist's companion in an age when [[automobiles]] were still a novelty, and quietly became the arbiter of culinary reputation across [[Hallia]].
