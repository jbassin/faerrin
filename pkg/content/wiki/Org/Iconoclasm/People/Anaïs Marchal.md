Anaïs Marchal is a late 20s human male, with red hair and dark brown eyes. He's the fraternal twin of [[Anouk Marchal|Anouk]]. Anaïs often wears sharp, plain suits but adorns his fingers and wrists with wooden rings and bracelets. He has a moderately sized raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Anaïs is the more magically-inclined of the twins. His portion of the [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]] workshop is dominated by magical tomes and alchemical reagents. He tends to be more reserved than his sister.

Between Anaïs and her sister [[Anouk Marchal|Anouk]], no small amount of hard-won experience stands behind them — seasoned adventurers both, and formidable for it.
