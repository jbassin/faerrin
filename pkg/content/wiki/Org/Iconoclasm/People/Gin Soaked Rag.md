Gin Soaked Rag, or Ginny for short, appears as a 6-foot-tall dishrag wearing a three-piece suit. The arms of the suit float in the air and end in gloves with mobility, despite the fact that the dishrag only occupies the center of the suit. His face is formed by the dishrag scrunching up, and when he moves some liquid appears to bead at the edges.

Ginny is the [[Hearts|Heart]] that maintains the [[Sin and Tonic]] fane. He's friends with [[Illmari Vaino|Illmari]] and the rest of [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]], and is considered by most to be part of the team, despite his inability to leave his position behind the bar.

The [[Gin Soaked Rag]] is, in truth, [[Ginny]] — the [[Heart]] of the [[Sin and Tonic]], manifesting as a suit vest and dress pants with a twisted dish rag where a head ought to be, wringing and craning itself toward whoever has just walked in. Ginny works the bar and can turn a hand to cooking, though those with a preference for human food are better served waiting on [[Illmari]].
