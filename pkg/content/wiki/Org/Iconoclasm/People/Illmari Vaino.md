Illmari is a late-60s half-elf male, with black, slicked back hair and scars on his face. He has a raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Illmari is the current proprietor of the [[Sin and Tonic]] and the founder of the mercenary group [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]]. He normally serves as the cook at the establishment, while [[Ginny]] works the bar. He was one of the first to capitalize on hearing the [[Voidsong]], collecting like-minded people to investigate its source and meaning.
