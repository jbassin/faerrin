Illmari is a late-60s half-elf male, with black, slicked back hair and scars on his face. He has a raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Illmari is the current proprietor of the [[Sin and Tonic]] and the founder of the mercenary group [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]]. He was one of the first to capitalize on hearing the [[Voidsong]], collecting like-minded people to investigate its source and meaning.

The founder of [[Iconoclasm]], [[Illmari Vaino]] is better known around [[Sin and Tonic]] as a cook — a tension between those two identities that those who know him tend not to press on.
