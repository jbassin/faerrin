Anouk Marchal is a late 20s human female, with red hair and dark brown eyes. She's the fraternal twin of [[Anaïs Marchal|Anaïs]]. Anouk usually wears practical workshop jumpsuits, as anything fancier would likely burn or get stained by one of her experiments. She has a moderately sized raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Anouk is the more technologically-inclined of the twins. Her portion of the [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]] workshop is covered by multimeters, oscilloscopes, and other electrical accoutrement. She tends to be more outgoing than her brother.


### Contacts & Job Leads

Anouk maintains a Rolodex-like collection of index cards containing contacts and job leads, which she uses to refer people to work opportunities. Among her contacts is an old friend who runs [[Sableclutch]] Scrap — known as [[Copperjaw]] — who regularly hires couriers and other workers, preferring to engage individuals directly rather than through a courier service.
