Elias Ramsey is an elderly, dark-skinned dwarven male. He has wispy blonde-gray hair on his balding head, and he keeps a short-trimmed mustache and beard. He wears tweed suits with elbow patches, and keeps a small pair of reading glasses on a chain around his neck. He has a small raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Elias is the group curmudgeon, often doing things his way or not at all. Despite this, he serves as an excellent quartermaster for the group, able to locate things that had been well lost by the other members of [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]].
