Copperjaw is an elderly dwarf, estimated to be around 120 to 130 years old — at the end of middle age or the beginning of elderly by dwarven standards. His bottom jaw has been replaced with a crude copper prosthetic, which is the origin of his name.

He operates out of a scrapyard, where he has access to a Giant crusher and feels confident deterring interested parties who come looking for trouble. Outside of his scrapyard, however, he does not feel confident protecting valuable items.

### Work and Courier Jobs

Copperjaw offers work in two broad categories: guarding something or moving something, with moving jobs paying better at 100 gold pieces. He has been known to hire people — including children — to deliver important items or cases.

For delivery jobs, Copperjaw uses a locked metal suitcase fitted with a magic handcuff on its handle, used to secure the case to a courier. The suitcase is extraordinarily heavy — heavy enough that dropping it would make a small crater in the ground.

### Connections

Copperjaw is a named individual known to [[Org/Protectorate/index|Blackline]] operatives, who would be informed if a goblin of interest were caught.
