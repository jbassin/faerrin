Benjamin Grayson is a human male, approximately 30 years old in appearance. He has stark white hair and gray, slate-colored eyes. He has a large raiment of the [[Eternal Pulse|Pulse]].

Grayson is the current CEO of [[Org/Amber Call/index|Amber Call]], the foremost premium health insurance provider in [[Geography/Calaria/index|Calaria]]. He is a follower of the [[Eternal Pulse|Pulse]], and secretly is a high level necromancer. He currently resides in [[Nos Mortem]].
