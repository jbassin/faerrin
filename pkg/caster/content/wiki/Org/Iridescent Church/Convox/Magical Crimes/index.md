The Magical Crimes Convox is a department within the [[Org/Iridescent Church/index|Iridescent Church]] devoted to solving the unexplained and the weird when they negatively impact the [[Iridescent Host|Host's]] people. Some cases they've worked on previously have included a mysterious curse originating from the [[Verdant Expanse]] and an everlasting rain from excessive augury.

The convox is headquartered at 33 Rook's Crossing in the [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/Aurideon/index|Aurideon]].
