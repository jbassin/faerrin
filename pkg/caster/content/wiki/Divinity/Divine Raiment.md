The Gods bless their followers with great power, so much so that it overflows one's mortal vessel into the air beyond. This phenomenon is known as a _raiment_ and usually manifests as rays of light surrounding one's head. In particularly blessed individuals, the effect is more acute -- Aurelia VII is said to be able to coat her whole body in the Host's light in the form of holy armor.

A raiment's color and sheen are derived from the deity that bestowed the blessing: followers of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]] tend to have pearlescent, shimmering raiments, while followers of the [[Judge of Ages|Judge]] have radiant golden raiments. The color and form allow for variations based on the individual, but generally speaking, one can tell how blessed (and by whom) someone is at a glance.
