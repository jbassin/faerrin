Flintlock Toboggan is a fiend kept in the [[Sigzil Haight|Shackled Prince's]] demesne. He's about 4'6", humanoid, covered in short brown fur. He has hooved feet, and a goatlike face with human features. He has two curled rams' horns protruding from his forehead, and his pupils are hourglass shaped instead of round.
