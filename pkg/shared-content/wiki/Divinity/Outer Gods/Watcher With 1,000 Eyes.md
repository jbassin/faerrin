The Watcher is the patron of [[Belvedere]] and all who look outward for answers in life. He teaches that the past is indelible and the future is inexorable; he entreats his followers to record every detail of what currently _is_, so that they might hasten progress towards what _will be_. The Watcher's adherents also spend ample time looking toward the night sky, as it's said that epiphanies come to those who understand their place in the greater world.

**Category** :: Outer God <br />
**Edicts** :: Chronicle existence, uncover hidden mysteries, advance society <br />
**Anathema** :: Destroy sources of knowledge, hinder forward technological progress <br />
**[[Divine Raiment]]** :: Pure white, twinkling <br />
**[[Celestial Prescence]]** :: Nebulae
