The Aurideon is the seat of power for the [[Org/Iridescent Church/index|Iridescent Church]]. One of two sovereign city-states within the bounds of [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/index|Hallia]], the Aurideon is governed exclusively by the Church, while the rest of the [[Geography/Calaria/index|Calarian]] government has little say. Entry is restricted for non-believers, but exceptions are frequently made when business requires it.

The Aurideon is covered in a giant crystal dome, structured to amplify the [[Iridescent Host|Host's]] [[Celestial Prescence|aurorae]] overhead. Most convox in the city are headquartered here.
