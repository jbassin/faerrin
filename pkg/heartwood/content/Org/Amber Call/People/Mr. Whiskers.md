Mr. Whiskers was a black and white mottled catfolk. He used to live on the streets of [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/Sableclutch/index|Sableclutch]], and was a proud member of the Anti-[[The Zorbon|Zorbon]] Taskforce, which met in the sewers underneath [[Geography/Calaria/Hallia/Widdershins/index|Widdershins]]. He was slain and used in a necromantic ritual by [[Benjamin Grayson]].
