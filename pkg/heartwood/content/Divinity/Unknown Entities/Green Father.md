The Green Father is an entity that resides within the [[Verdant Expanse]]. Little is known about the Green Father, other than that it's believed he encourages the agricultural cultivation of arable lands.

**Category** :: Inner God <br />
**Edicts** :: Tend to the land, grow new and strange flora <br />
**Anathema** :: Tear down existing plantlife, disrupt the natural order <br />
**[[Divine Raiment]]** :: Green, floral <br />
**[[Celestial Prescence]]** :: Occasional green flares
