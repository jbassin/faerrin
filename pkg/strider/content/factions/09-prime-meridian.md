Founded as a modest transportation company, Prime Meridian leveraged infrastructure contracts to become the dominant road-and-rail operator across Færrin—eventually collaborating with the Iridescent Church to peacefully dissolve the Calarian monarchy. They now operate as a cornerstone institution under the resulting Council government.

## Known Members

### Tas Millett

Tas Millett is a tall, regal hobgoblin with flawless slate-gray skin and sharp, chiseled features, dressed in a meticulously pressed naval coat traced with gold-trimmed cartography lines and an antique brass astrolabe at his belt.
