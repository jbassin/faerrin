An armored transport-and-security firm that occupies "the line between upstanding armored transport and hooligans with too-fast cars," based in the Termean Veinlands in Fenrith. Clients receive a live scry of their transport throughout the job; the crew blends automotive engineers, mechanics, and armed security who engage threats directly rather than avoid them.

## Known Members

### Ceres

Ceres is a sharp-eyed, athletic fetchling with monochromatic grayscale skin and hair that absorbs local light, wearing an oil-stained duster over formal wear with a pair of heavily customized, heavy-frame hand cannons visible beneath her coat.
