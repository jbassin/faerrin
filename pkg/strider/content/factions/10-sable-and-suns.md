A military contractor that distinguishes itself by pairing weapons and vehicle sales with on-site consultants who teach clients how to use them. Ideologically neutral, S&S openly supplies all sides—state militaries and pirate kingdoms alike—and their branded arms serve as a reliable signal of serious financial backing.

## Known Members

### Lark Crowe

Lark Crowe is a tengu with glossy black feathers and an intense scar cracking his beak vertically in half. He wears leather vests and rugged denim common with old scouts from Austrene.
