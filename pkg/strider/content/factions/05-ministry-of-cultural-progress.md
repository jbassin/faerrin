The Ministry of Cultural Progress owns the right to broadcast on the radio spectrum in Calaria and Tormeré. They manage broadcasting channels, curate music stations, and produce radio dramas and screamsheet editorials. Listening to MoCuP stations is a popular pasttime across the country.

## Known Members

Giannos Hasikos is an average looking half-elf with nondescript features and a mundane bearing. He exists.
