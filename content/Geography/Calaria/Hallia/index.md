Hallia is a city in [[Geography/Calaria/index|Calaria]] divided into numbered districts, connected by a public transit network that residents are required to use for inter-district travel.

### Districts & Layout

Hallia is organized into numbered districts (e.g., Star 12) and oriented along a voidward–zenithward axis. The voidward end of the city passes through slums before giving way to undeveloped land that feels like the far outskirts despite being geographically close to the city center. Factories are concentrated in the voidward strata, situated there to minimize legal liability and harm to residents; many dump waste into the river that runs through the city.

The **Masque District** is a more affluent area containing hotels and catering to tourists.

[[Sableclutch]] is a neighborhood located near the party's base of operations.

### Transportation

Particularly strong magic set up by the host prevents residents from teleporting or sliding freely between city districts; all inter-district travel must be made via public transit.

Hallia's primary public transit network is a trolley (tram) system that runs along a fixed route called **the Horizon**, connecting the city's numbered districts. Trams arrive periodically at designated stops. Stations follow a numbered or tiered naming convention — known stops include **Void 2** and **Zero** (also called **Prime**). A trolley station serves the voidward industrial district near the factories and scrapyards.

### Points of Interest

- **Sableclutch Scrap** — a scrapyard in the voidward industrial district, characterized by mountains of trash and large metal-crushing machinery. Accessible by tram.

### Law Enforcement

**[[Org/Black Line/index|Black Line]]** (also written *Blackline*) is a private law enforcement company that polices at least some districts of the city, generally operating in the nicer, more affluent areas. Black Line officers are considered unusual when present in less affluent parts of the city.
