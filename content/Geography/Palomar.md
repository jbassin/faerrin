---
title: Palomar
---
A fort located to the east of [[Karwin]], formerly known by a different name before being renamed to Palomar.

On regional maps, Palomar appears to the right of [[Karwin]], with a mountain range roughly halfway up the map, [[Trilene]] marked nearby, and the beginning of a body of water visible on the far west side of the map.

> [!note] Former Name
> Palomar was formerly known by a different name; the old name has been obscured on at least one known map.
