A battered dwarven man of late middle age — by dwarven reckoning, somewhere around 120–130 years old. His most distinctive feature is his bottom jaw, which has been replaced with a crude copper prosthetic roughly shaped like a jaw, the origin of his name. His nose is mashed-up and misshapen, and he frequently sports a black eye, both signs of a man who gets into fights regularly.

Copperjaw operates a scrapyard, where he keeps a Giant Crusher — both a working tool and a deterrent to unwanted visitors. He is not the sort to make allowances for age when sizing someone up; his handshake alone makes that clear.

He has been known to hire people, including children, to deliver important items or cases on his behalf. His name is known to [[Org/Protectorate/index|Blackline]] agents, who have reason to keep track of him.
