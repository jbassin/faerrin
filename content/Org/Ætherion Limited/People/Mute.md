Mute is the name given by the party to one of two shapeshifting figures, the other being [[Smith]], encountered together. In their disguised form they appear unremarkable, but are capable of growing approximately a foot and a half in size and shedding their disguised appearance during transformation, at which point their clothes melt into their bodies.

In their true form, Mute and Smith appear as large, pink, fleshy, naked mole rat-like creatures. Their facial features largely melt back into the skin, with the eyes sinking into the head and leaving only empty sockets.
