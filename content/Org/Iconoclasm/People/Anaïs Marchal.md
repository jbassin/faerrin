Anaïs Marchal is a late 20s human male, with red hair and dark brown eyes. He's the fraternal twin of [[Anouk Marchal|Anouk]]. Anaïs often wears sharp, plain suits but adorns his fingers and wrists with wooden rings and bracelets. He has a moderately sized raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Anaïs is the more magically-inclined of the twins. His portion of the [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm]] workshop is dominated by magical tomes and alchemical reagents. The twins do not run a magic item shop — they are simply two people who happen to be particularly skilled at crafting. He tends to be more reserved than his sister.
