Josh (josh.r.bassin@gmail.com / "Josh Bassin") owns and maintains the Heart of Hearts
TTRPG campaign wiki (heart.iridi.cc) effectively solo.

Stated priorities for the refactor work:
- The site "stays a reading wiki" — not heading toward an app.
- Solid is "a means to an end, not a goal." He is wary of adopting tech for its own sake.
- Wants OFF the vendored Quartz fork primarily to reduce long-term maintenance burden.

How to apply: Favor scope discipline and total-cost-of-ownership framing. Push back on
complexity that only pays off in an app-shaped future he explicitly disclaimed. See
[[astro-refactor]].
