Letov Obratz was a dapper purple-skinned tiefling with small, polished obsidian horns, wearing an incredibly complex multi-layered suit of shifting, iridescent fabrics that change texture with his mood.

Letov is a member of [[Org/Garrick Textiles/index|Garrick Textiles]].
