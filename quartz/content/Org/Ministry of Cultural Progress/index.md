The Ministry of Cultural Progress (MoCuP) owns the right to broadcast on the radio spectrum in [[Geography/Calaria/index|Calaria]] and [[Geography/Tormeré/index|Tormeré]]. They manage broadcasting channels, curate music stations, and produce radio dramas and screamsheet editorials. Listening to MoCuP stations is a popular pastime across the country.
