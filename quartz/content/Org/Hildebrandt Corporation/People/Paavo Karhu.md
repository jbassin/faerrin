Paavo Karhu is a massive, elderly winterkin changeling with skin like cracked ice and a snow-white beard, dressed in a heavy tailored velvet overcoat with diamond-buttoned lapels and moving slowly with a silver-headed cane.

Paavo is a member of [[Org/Hildebrandt Corporation/index|Hildebrandt Corporation]].
