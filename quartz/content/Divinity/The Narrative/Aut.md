Aut is a humanoid with completely white skin. He has oversized hands and feet, but unnaturally gangly, thin arms and legs. His torso is a simple cylinder. His head is perfectly spherical, overly large, and has absolutely no facial features expect for a giant toothy grin that stretches from one side of his face to the other.

He makes a habit of observing the world, and nudging it in the direction he prefers through his "friends." The recent advent of the [[Voidsong]] has somehow disrupted his ability, however, much to his chagrin. He lives somewhere within the [[Geography/Black and White/index|Black and White]].
