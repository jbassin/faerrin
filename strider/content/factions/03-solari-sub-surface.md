Solari Sub-surface is a small-size shipping company, that works to fill the gaps left in the market by larger players like Prime Meridian and Alkahest Freight.

## Known Members

### Salvado Tarragona

Salvado Tarragona is a short, stocky, densely muscled man whose leathery skin bears the scars of a long career underground and whose bloated eyes betrays a lifetime drowning in booze. He dresses in a half-formal, half-flight-suit ensemble fastened with brass buckles.
