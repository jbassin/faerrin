The Zorbon is a supposed entity that controls the minds of the unsuspecting. His machinations can be repelled by tinfoil, and so those that try to avoid his influence fashion clothing, like hats, out of foil. He's been described has having fungal qualities.

It's believed that the Zorbon is somehow related to [[Benjamin Grayson|Grayson]], the CEO of [[Org/Amber Call/index|Amber Call]].
