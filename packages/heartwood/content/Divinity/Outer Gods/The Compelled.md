The Compelled was the patron deity of the [[Geography/Raelion/index|Raelion]] Empire but is now worshipped by the few Raelish refugees on the continent. His teachings focus on a form of predestination -- that all have an ideal life, and it's a follower's utmost responsibility to reach that ideal. The Compelled also emphasizes both meritocratic hierarchies and respect for those higher than oneself in said hierarchy.

**Category** :: Outer God <br />
**Edicts** :: Achieve your best self, work hard, and demand others do as well <br />
**Anathema** :: Disobey a superior, shirk your duties <br />
**[[Divine Raiment]]** :: Silver, matte <br />
**[[Celestial Prescence]]** :: Meteor shower
