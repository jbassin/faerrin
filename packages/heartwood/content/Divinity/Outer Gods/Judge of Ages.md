The Judge of Ages is the patron of [[The Scale]] and styles himself as the empowered intermediary between the other Outer Gods. He teaches his followers to act as impartial adjudicators for conflicts between other religions and strives to maintain balance in the world. Particularly fervent interpretations of his teachings imply that without the stabilizing force of the Judge, the world would fall into unbalanced chaos.

**Category** :: Outer God <br />
**Edicts** :: Perform just executions, study local laws, oppose corrupt or bloodthirsty government officials <br />
**Anathema** :: Kill without thought, execute the innocent, mock the condemned, falsely incriminate another <br />
**[[Divine Raiment]]** :: Golden, radiant <br />
**[[Celestial Prescence]]** :: Ring from horizon to horizon
