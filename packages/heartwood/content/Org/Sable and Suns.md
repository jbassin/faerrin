Sable and Suns is a military contractor that goes the extra mile. Where other companies might sell you weapons and vehicles, S&S will send a consultant to teach you how to use them. Technically unaffiliated with the countries of Edan, S&S openly plays all sides. They're one of the major contractors that supply the [[Geography/Calaria/index|Calarian]] military, but [[Protectorate]], the Pirate-Kings of [[Geography/Lorandris/index|Lorandris]], and some up-and-coming [[Org/index|Orgs]] out of [[Geography/Rhædon/index|Rhædon]] all hold contracts with them.

When dealing with mercenary gangs and bandits, if their weaponry is of S&S make, that's a telltale sign that someone with money is backing them. Sable and Suns firearms and blades are high quality but don't come cheap.
