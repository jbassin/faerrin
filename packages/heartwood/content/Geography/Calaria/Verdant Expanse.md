An ocean of trees, a thousand miles wide, sits between Calaria and the Tenchwin Sea. Trees a mile tall choke the sunslight from the sky, and many a strange creature prowls its darkened depths. The Green Sea violently resists outside subjugation, and the modern man has all but given up on delving into its deepest regions.
