An extranational governing body serving the Judge of Ages, imposing order and balance across Færrin through four ranked divisions—Pactbrokers, Arbiters, Overseers, and Timekeepers. They enforce magically-binding contracts and operate from a stronghold in the Opaline Mountains.

## Known Members

### Ralph Bishop

Ralph Bishop is a heavily scarred, golden-scaled lizardfolk who wears a tailored but slightly too-tight silk waistcoat.

### Lance Vesper

Lance Vesper is a lithe, sickly human with pale skin and rheumy eyes. He wears a high-collared dark leather coat lined with hidden pockets.

### Cassian Drake

Cassian Drake is a burly, heavily tattooed human with a broken nose and a shaved head. He wears tactical greaves and carries a long-barreled rifle.
