One of the oldest organizations on Færrin, predating the Gods themselves—a society of necromancers and healers endorsed by the Outer God known as the Eternal Pulse. They maintain hospitals, research facilities, and Columbariums where wealthy patrons donate their bodies as preserved artworks while the Society pursues its ultimate goal of true resurrection.

## Known Members

### Manus Sudrenus

Manus Sudrenus is a sleek, high-elven aristocrat with silver hair tied back in a silken ribbon, dressed in flowing dark silk robes.
