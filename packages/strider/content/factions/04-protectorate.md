A private military organization originating from Brithwyn—a mercenary force-for-hire that now also functions as domestic peacekeepers within the nation. Their elite soldier caste, the slayers, are trained to enter a heightened battletrance state with devastating effect.

## Known Members

### Tamara McKay

Tamara McKay is a female elf in high-ranking security armor who usually keeps her face masked, though her movements are so fluid and relaxed they communicate everything a face would. If she unmasks, a couple of distinct, elegant dueling scars mark her jaw.

### Yao Addington

Yao Addington is a male gnome encased in heavy, oversized tactical plating that clanks with every step, his face hidden behind an intimidating stylized visor.
