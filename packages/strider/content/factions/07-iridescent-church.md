The most powerful organization on Færrin, founded to spread the teachings of the Iridescent Host under the eternal leadership of Pontifex Aurelia (currently Aurelia VII, believed to be a perpetual reincarnation). The Church functions as part of Calarian state government and holds significant sway across Brithwyn and Tormeré.

## Known Members

### Walter Stoke

Walter Stoke is an elderly, blind human priest whose heavy, layered vestments are woven from translucent, prismatic glass fibers that catch and scatter the light.
