## Known Members

### The Master of Ceremonies

The Master of Ceremonies is a tall, slender elf in an elaborate high-collared coat adorned with a striking blue-and-gold diamond harlequin pattern, his face concealed behind a pale, unblinking mask with small elegant horns.
