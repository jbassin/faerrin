Dura Oil-Drinker is a mid-40s orcish woman, with dark green skin. She has two tusks on her bottom jaw, but one has been cut off and sanded to a stump. She has curly black hair, and wears mechanics overalls and dark welding goggles. She has a small raiment of the [[Iridescent Host|Host]].

Dura is [[Org/Iconoclasm/index|Iconoclasm's]] resident gearhead. She maintains the company car, handles contacts with outside organizations, and manages the group ledger. She was the first to join Iconoclasm back when [[Illmari Vaino|Illmari]] founded it.
