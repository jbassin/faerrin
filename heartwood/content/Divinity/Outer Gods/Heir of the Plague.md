The Heir of the Plague is the patron of no countries, but her worshippers can be found everywhere in small quantities. She teaches her followers to respect (and, in some cases, enforce) the cycle of life and the inevitability of death. While her domain encompasses life and death, her reputation is most closely tied to the waves of pestilence that frequently take the weak and infirm before their time.

**Category** :: Outer God <br />
**Edicts** :: Abide by the cycles of life, aid childbirth, destroy undead <br />
**Anathema** :: Strive to break the cycle of life (such as by attaining immortality or creating undead), despoil the earth, kill a juvenile creature <br />
**[[Divine Raiment]]** :: Deep red, firey <br />
**[[Celestial Prescence]]** :: Bleeding moon
