## Overview
Stakeholders were given access to the current build to see the output and their feedback was pretty negative.
There were two major issues:

### Issue 1: Extraneous Information

The most glaring issue was the info that was being pulled out of the transcripts was, for the most part, unnecessary. The wiki is supposed to hold information on descriptions of people, places, and things, with perhaps basic overviews of their history if important. However, most of the facts pulled out of the transcripts are useless for that task, like "Illmari promised the group food" or "Benny rolled an 8". The wiki isn't supposed to be a summary of the transcripts, and definitely doesn't need any info from combat.

### Issue 2: Data Reliability

Another issue the stakeholders had was they didn't like the `mixed` blocks. They would prefer that if a block is mixed, it's broken down into smaller `ic` and `occ` segments, since they don't want a human to have to review all of the `mixed` blocks.

Also, a lot of the info coming from the players (and speculative info in general) isn't great. They'd like to change the system to only ever include info from the gm, or info from the players that is directly corroborated by the gm. This should also reduce human interaction in the system.

### This ticket

We want to scope out the work to address the stakeholder concerns before we continue implementing the remaining tickets. At the time of writing, the repo has completed the first 8 tickets.
