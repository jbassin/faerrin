Rhædon is the forgotten black sheep of Edan. Separated on land by the deadly-hot Siksithys desert and on the sea by the turbulent and jagged Tenchwin cape, for a long time, Rhædon was utterly isolated. Before airframes and easy flight, it was rare to see a Rhædinan outside of their home country -- the trek was too dangerous to be worth it. However, now that one can bypass the most dangerous parts of the journey, the country is starting to enter the global stage.

Rhædon might not wield the same power as its contemporaries on the continent, but its people are nothing if not determined. In recent years, Rhædon has been reaching across the continent to insert itself into formerly stable trade -- willing to go much lower than competitors to get their foot in the door. They hit a major setback when their staunchest ally was bombed out of existence, but they haven't spent the past forty years doing nothing.

Historically, Rhædon couldn't trade with others and needed to be self-sufficient. As such, much of the population worked in some agrarian field -- farming or ranching. This is no longer true with the world getting smaller as it has been. Businesses span the desert, and young folk migrate towards the rest of the continent, picking up odd jobs along the way. [[Org/index|Orgs]] have moved manufacturing to Rhædon to capitalize on lower wages, and the country has shifted away from its agricultural roots.
